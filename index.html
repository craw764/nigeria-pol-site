About the Honourable

Kene Nnadi

Member, House of Representatives | 10th National Assembly

At 38, Hon. Kene Nnadi stands as one of Nigeria's most energetic and results-driven legislators — a product of Ikorodu's streets, schools, and markets, now championing the constituency's future at the highest level of federal lawmaking.

Born and raised in Ikorodu, Lagos State, Kene rose through merit — earning his Bachelor's degree in Political Science from the University of Lagos and a Master's in Public Policy from the University of Ibadan. Before politics, he built a career in community development and youth empowerment, founding the Ikorodu Youth Forward Initiative which trained over 5,000 young people in vocational skills.

A Life of Service

Youth Activist

Local Government Councillor

Elected Councillor, Ikorodu LGA. Championed infrastructural development and educational grants.

2019

First Election to House of Reps

Elected to the 9th National Assembly with 68% of votes, becoming the youngest Rep from Lagos State.

2023

Re-elected — 10th Assembly

Re-elected with an overwhelming 74% mandate. Appointed Chairman, Committee on Youth Development & Sports.

Driving legislation that unlocks economic opportunities for young Nigerians — from startup grants to federal job placement programmes in Ikorodu.

Infrastructure & Roads

Advocating aggressively for federal roads, bridges, drainage, and electricity projects that connect Ikorodu to Lagos and the national grid.

  • Ikorodu-Sagamu road dualisation
  • 3 new bridges approved
  • ₦8B power project

Healthcare Access

Sponsoring bills to bring federal medical facilities, essential drug supply, and maternal health programmes to underserved communities in Ikorodu.

  • 2 federal PHCs upgraded
  • Free maternal care programme
  • Mobile health outreach

Environment & Climate

Leading the charge on flood control, waterway sanitation, and clean energy adoption across Lagos communities through targeted federal legislation.

  • Flood relief intervention bill
  • Ikorodu waterways clean-up
  • Solar energy pilot project

Security & Rule of Law

Securing federal resources for police posts, surveillance infrastructure, and community policing frameworks to make Ikorodu safe for families and businesses.
